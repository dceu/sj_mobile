RENTAL APPLICATION: If you are asked, there are three options to fill out the rental application. Each adult needs to fill out a separate application, although common information need only be placed on one of the applications.

  • This online form,
  • Copy the text from this page into an email to sjapartments@gmail.com and fill in the information within the email.

  • Paper form (least preferred, but acceptable) which you can download, complete by hand, and then either scan in and email to us at sjapartments@gmail.com, or hand deliver to us.
