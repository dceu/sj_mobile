Contact Information

When initially contacting us, please complete the Preliminary information form at the bottom of this page. This allows us to (a) save time and thus NOT charge an application fee. (b) ensure you're inquiring about a unit suitable for you, (c) recommend other units consistent with your needs, and (d) screen out the 30% of our responses which are scams, spam or from those definitely financially unqualified. Our reply back to you and all future messages between us will be via the email you provide. Also note the following:

  • We usually require that accounts with the power and, if applicable, water companies be in the names of one or more persons whose name is on the lease. If you need an exception to this, you need to tell us before viewing our houses or apartments.
  • Deposit policy: We typically, with an occasional exception, require a deposit equal to one month's rent and the first month's rent paid in full at lease signing. (Those receiving government rent subsidies need pay the deposit plus their share of first month's rent.)
  • For the 3-5% of respondents who in the last ten years have one or more evictions or eviction cases filed against them, must explain the circumstances that led to each of these. Prior evictions do not necessarily disqualify you (Online public records are checked to confirm your answers about evictions. Inaccurate answers or omissions will disqualify you)
